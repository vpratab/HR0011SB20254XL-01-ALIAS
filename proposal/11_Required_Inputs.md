# Required Inputs

These items are not fully derivable from the repo and must be assembled by the company before submission.

## Company / Portal Data

- legal company name
- UEI / SAM registration status
- CAGE code
- DSIP account and submission contacts
- business address and points of contact

## Team Data

- PI legal name, resume, citizenship/work status
- key technical staff resumes
- subcontractor identities, roles, and contacts
- signed commitment or teaming letters if used

## Cost Volume Inputs

- direct labor categories and hours
- loaded labor rates
- indirect rates / basis
- travel assumptions
- materials / cloud / software costs
- subcontractor cost detail
- fee / profit assumptions if allowed

## Compliance / Disclosure Inputs

- foreign national participation, if any
- export-control / ITAR handling statement
- FOCI / affiliation disclosures if required
- human or animal use status, if applicable

## Transition / Commercial Inputs

- target government program or customer
- transition contact or surrogate platform target
- commercialization report inputs
- relevant past performance or prior awards

## Topic-Specific Inputs For This Repo

- confirmed official track status for **HR0011SB20254XL-01**
- final page-limit and portal-format check
- any topic-specific attachments required by the component instructions
